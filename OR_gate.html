Logical OR Gate

An OR Gate is another logic gate commonly used in classical computing.

Try clicking on the bits on the right side, then click the yellow OR gate and watch what you get for the outcome on the left side. Try this a few times and see if you notice a pattern.

You should notice that if either the first or the second bits are 1 then the result will be 1 and 0 otherwise.
